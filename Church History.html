OUR HISTORY

A journey of faith, grace and growth

Spring of Shiloh Int’l Church was established in 2020 and was inspired by  inspired by a divine encounter drawn from Judges 15:18–20, where Samson, after a great victory, found himself weary and thirsty. In his distress, he cried out to God, and the Lord opened a spring of water for him, bringing strength and renewal.

Mission

To preach the gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture believers, and impact communities through love and service.

Vision

To be a fountain of spiritual renewal where lives are transformed through the Word and presence of God.
